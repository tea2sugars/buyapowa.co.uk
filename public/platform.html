The Buyapowa platform

Buyapowa is a social commerce business. We think social should be making you money, not costing it. Our unique social commerce platform gets your social audience shopping, by giving them a genuine level of control alongside real entertainment and genuine social currency. Our tools allow you to engage and transact socially; to deliver the experience across multiple pages and social networks; to control and influence the messaging around your products; and to build a rich picture of your social customers and the impact of social commerce on your business.

Social commerce

People love systems with a bit of fun to them: a game, a competition, a countdown. We also love taking on the system, getting something for nothing and gaining social kudos. These are the human truths that already drive social media networks – the ones that cost brands money to inhabit. Buyapowa takes them all and sets them to work. Same impulses, different outcome. Great trick if you can do it. Six different models, one driving principle: the patterns that drive social media uptake can drive profit.

Transactions

SHARE-MORE-GET-MORE: The value of the deal increases as more shoppers sign up. Buyers get more for their money as more people join
PRICE DROP: The offline model of co-operative shopping to create waves of sharing through gamification
CASHBACK: Rewards shoppers for getting their friends to buy the same product, through cash back incentives

Engagements

THE TIPPING POINT : Designed to generate leads as customers sign up to hit a tipping point and trigger the offer
THE VOTE: More lead generation as we encourage consumers to vote for the deal that they want to go live
MEGAPHONE: Rewarding shoppers for telling potential buyers about their purchases. Leverages gamification to create noise

Social plugins

Our experiences are designed so they can take place wherever your desired audiences reside. Spread widgets across your blogger network, or reward followers on Facebook with an app. We work closely with all the major networks, and we’re always adding emerging platforms into the mix – and mature channels such as email. And the experience is designed to grow across multiple social networks. Seed it in Facebook or Twitter, and then watch it grow across Tumblr and Pinterest. And our responsive design ensures the experience is optimised based on the network in which it surfaces.

Social CRM

You’ll generate transactions, awareness, engagement, and a whole lot of audience touch points. Connect with your customer at the right time in the right context. Create, schedule, route and personalize messages across multiple social networks and pages. Interact with your audience in ways that are most relevant to them. Create targeting presets based on language and geography, enabling regional localization for global brands. Measure the transaction, referrals and visits that your activity generates. Then optimize your messaging based on which actions drive engagement.

Social Insight

We help you understand the real impact of social on your bottom line. With social commerce you’ll far more data you’re used to with traditional commerce. Imagine the possibilities: study the influence of each engagement or analyse larger trends. Track referral sources, view demographics data and generate custom reports. Understand the cost of acquisition, the effectiveness of your buyers as marketers, and calculate the true value of social commerce. Inject data into your own CRM tools.
