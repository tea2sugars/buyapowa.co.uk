The three stages of
Social Maturity

Where are you? | Where are they? | What next?

Companies like Tesco, J&J and PepsiCo are already getting their social fans and followers shopping, generating direct sales from social. Why Aren't you?

Having worked with with over 50 leading brands, including the likes of Sony, Argos, Pfizer and even Robbie Williams, the experts at Buyapowa have mapped the evolution of social media, identifying three distinct stages along a social maturity index.

Take the survey > Download Report >

We'll explore each of these in greater detail but, to summarise, they are:

Stage 1: Attract Fans & Followers

Grab attention and build your numbers by broadcasting relevant material and staging competitions, polls, quizzes, etc.

+ Engage your audience in order to understand them, build a dialogue and foster a community

Stage 2: Engage your community

Develop a powerful community by interacting with your audience and offering them insider privileges.

With co-creation, crowdsourcing and influencer activation.

+ Invite your community to request products and offers and allow those offers improve as people shop.

Stage 3: Build a Social Sales Channel

Realise the value of social and generate measurable ROI by turning fans and followers into shoppers. Enable this with Co-buys, gamification and dynamic pricing.
