Six different co-buying models, one driving principle;
Get your social audience shopping.

  • Share More Get More:

  • The value of the deal increases as more shoppers sign up. Buyers get more for their money as more people join.

  • Price Drop:

  • The offline model of co-operative shopping to create waves of sharing through gamification.

  • Cashback:

  • Rewards shoppers for getting their friends to buy the same products through cash back incentives.

  • The tipping point:

  • Designed to generate leads as customers sign up to hit a tipping point and trigger the offer.

  • The Vote:

  • More lead generation as we encourage consumers to vote for the deal that they want to go live.

  • Megaphone:

  • Rewarding shoppers for telling potential buyers about their purchases. Leverages gamification to create noise.
